üìñ About This Project

This is a validation system (Phase 1) for standardizing amphibian road mortality data collection. Volunteers help salamanders and other amphibians cross roads safely during breeding migrations, and this tool makes it easy to collect consistent, high-quality data.

How it works:

  1. During patrol: Use Field Form on your phone to log observations quickly
  2. After patrol: Use Detail Form to add road details and additional notes
  3. Data is stored in Google Sheets for easy analysis and sharing

Tips:

  • Bookmark the Field Form on your phone's home screen for quick access
  • The Field Form works offline - data will sync when you're back online
  • Use the same observer name in both forms so you can find your data
